Ways to Give

The following options explain ways to give if you feel lead to do so. God loves a cheerful giver, but do not feel pressured to give.

Giving Boxes

Check

Online

Giving boxes are located in the main foyer. There are boxes on the east and west side of the foyer. Cash or checks are accepted. If you need help locating them, stop by the Welcome Center or ask any of our pastors or deacons.

Envelopes for checks can be found in each pew or at the welcome desk. Visit our office to get a set of 12 envelopes.

To give online, go to Simple Church to make a single donation or set up a recurring donation. If you need any help with this process, do not hesitate to reach out to one of our pastors or staff.
