Our Mission

Tri-City Baptist Church exists to worship God by loving Him supremely and loving others joyfully.
We endeavor to be a family-oriented ministry that is concerned about nurturing relationships from the youngest to the oldest within the institution that God has ordained and established.

We strive to have "a H.E.A.R.T. for God and others":

H   Honoring the Lord

E   Exposition the Word

A   Affection for Others

R   Reaching the World

T   Training the Church

...Thou shalt love the Lord thy God with all thy heart,
and with all thy mind; and thy neighbor as thyself.
Matthew 22:37-39
