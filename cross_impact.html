Cross Impact Campus Ministries

Cross Impact exists to reach college students at various universities across the campus with the Gospel. There are multiple chapters across the United States - from California to Colorado to Indiana. Tri-City is the national headquarters for Cross Impact. The local chapter in Colorado was formed at the University of Colorado Boulder (UC Boulder). The most recent chapter was founded at Colorado School of Mines by Pastor Senn.
