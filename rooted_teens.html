ROOTED Teens

ROOTED Teens is Tri-City's teen group led by Pastor Steadman. The purpose of the groups is to be ROOTED in Christ and for every part of one's life to be changed by Christ. Pastor Steadman established the name of the teen group when he joined the church back in 2017 and it has been a flourishing ministry at Tri-City. Colossians 2:6-7 is the group's verse.

"As you received Christ Jesus the Lord, so walk in Him
ROOTED and built up in Him and established in tthe faith,
abounding in it with thanksgiving." Colossians 2:6-7
