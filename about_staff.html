Our Staff

Dr. William Senn - Senior Pastor

Pastor Larry Robbins - Senior Adults Pastor

Pastor Nathan Steadman - Teens and Outreach Pastor

Dr. Bryan Malik - Children and Missions Pastor

Pastor Senn has been pastoring at Tri-City Baptist Church for over 20 years. He has his Master of Divinity, Doctorate of Ministyr and honorary Doctorate of Divinity. He is the founder and president of Cross Impact Campus Ministries and is invovled on severla colelge and mission boards.

Pastor Robbins has been a part of Tri-City since 1983. He has served as the music pastor but now serves as the "Young at Heart" senior adult pastor. He also serves in the community as the City of Westminster Police Chaplin, ministering to almost 250 individuals. Pastor Robbins has endorsements from Chaplain and Counseling agencies in addition to his Bachelors and Masters degree.

Pastor Steadman came to Tri-City in 2017 as the teens and outreach pastor. He has his Masters in Pastoral Studies and Bachelors in Bible. Him, his wife Janelle, and their three sons moved to Westminster from Kansas City, MO.

Pastor Bryan and his family were missionaries in Lithuania and Puerto Rico before joining Tri-City in 2010. He has received his master of Arts and Doctor of Divinity in Pastoral Studies. Him and his wife, Karen, work with the children on Wednesday nights and direct bi-annual children's plays.
